Support

Get in Touch

If you have questions, feedback, feature requests, or need to report a bug, please contact us via email:

support@barretina-software.de

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

What to Include

When contacting support, please include:

  • App name: Which application you're using
  • Device information: iPhone model and iOS version
  • Issue description: What happened and what you expected
  • Screenshots: If applicable, to help us understand the issue

Feedback Welcome

Your feedback helps improve these applications. Whether you have a feature suggestion, found something confusing, or just want to share your experience, we'd love to hear from you.
